What is IndieWebClub?

IndieWebClub is part of a growing world-wide network of meetups for everyone who wants to take back their web experience from social media silos, and own their online identities & content, or just want support with blogging!

  • 📝 Want to blog more and looking for: ideas, momentum, and a supportive community?
  • 📅 Join a gathering of like-minded people and get friendly support with writing, creating, and anything related to using and improving your own website!
  • 👩🏽‍💻 You do not need to have any tech background or experience in having your own website. You can even start with a Google Doc or your local notes app!

The IndieWebClub Bangalore community organizes in-person meetups at a roughly two-week cadence in the city of Bangalore, India.

You can join us on WhatsApp or Discourse.

Agenda

Do you enjoy reading or writing reviews for the media you engage with (books/movies/games) on your blog? Why or why not? What makes for a good review? What do you accomplish by writing one? What are you looking for when you read a review of something? How do reviews work differently on the web vs. in print? Do they still have value in a world of abundance? Do they increase your enjoyment of the art you’re engaging with?

Following from the above, what do you feel about being overly critical, mean, or downright inflammatory in your writing on the web? It can be easy to get page views by writing something clearly inflammatory, but do you want to put your name on a post that comes off as angry or frustrated? What if your anger is justified? What if it’s valuable to write that post? How do you call something out without seeming like a person who just complains about things? How do you criticize a work of art/company/product/person constructively?

As usual, we will end the meetup with a prompt and a writing session. Bring a device you can use to write and post to your blog.
